# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to keeping this project welcoming and respectful for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Accept different experience levels and perspectives.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants when behavior is harmful to the project community.

Report concerns by email to `manhducdongyb@gmail.com`.
